Chapter 00

This chapter provides the public entry point to THE KINETIC ENTERPRISE. It introduces the core failure mode the doctrine addresses and orients the reader to the structure of the work.

The doctrine exists to explain why execution degrades as decision latency exceeds environmental tempo, and why traditional governance mechanisms amplify this failure.
